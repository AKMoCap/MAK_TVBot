# CLAUDE.md

## Custom Commands

### /optimizecode
Review the codebase for bugs and suggest improvements, with a focus on:
- **User Experience (UX)**: Identify UI/UX issues, accessibility concerns, and usability improvements
- **Error Handling**: Find missing or inadequate error handling, edge cases, and failure scenarios
- **Site Performance**: Analyze APIs, websockets, and general load speed for optimization opportunities
